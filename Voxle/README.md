# Voxle

A 3D navigation game built with Three.js. Guide your orange cube through a floating field of blue cubes, collect green cubes to increase your score.

## Controls

- **W** or **↑** – Move forward
- **S** or **↓** – Move backward
- **A** or **←** – Strafe left
- **D** or **→** – Strafe right

## Features

- **Player**: A small orange cube you control
- **Blue cubes**: Hundreds of smaller blue cubes drifting and flowing in 3D space
- **Green cubes**: Periodically spawn among the blue cubes; touch them to add to your score
- **Scoreboard**: Displayed at the top of the screen

## Running

Open `index.html` in a modern browser, or serve the folder with any static server.
